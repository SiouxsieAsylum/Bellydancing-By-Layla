About Me

Layla is a master of the American Cabaret style of the ancient art of bellydance. She studied under the legendary late Serena Wilson, matriarch of Serena Studios and Serena Dance Theater. A multi-level instructor, Layla teaches group classes, as well as private instruction with Finger Cymbals, Performance Preparation and Cabaret Karsilama, her workshop specialties. She has over thirty years of teaching experience throughout the NYC area.

Layla has been performing New York Cabaret- Style Belly Dance for over thirty-five years and has appeared in such prestigious venues as Lincoln Center, Riverside Church, Queens Central Library Concert Series, United Nations, and 92nd Street Y Harkness Center. Layla has performed in Egypt, Europe, Canada, and the Caribbean, as well as throughout the Tri-State Area and West Coast. She is currently the director of student troupe, Veiled Visions Dance Ensemble, as well as a member of professional dance company, Kismet Dance Ensemble.

Currently, Layla has partnered with Bellydance America to bring this beautiful art and glorious costuming to a wider audience of the NYC area. Her passion has shaped the careers of many a talented dancer, and has helped to shape the footprint of bellydance on New York’s City’s famous arts.
